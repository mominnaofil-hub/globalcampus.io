About Us

Jagdish Krishna Educational Society’s Global Campus is a visionary initiative offering education from Pre-Primary to Post Graduation under one roof. It proudly manages Global International High School & Junior College (Affiliated to Maharashtra State Board) and Global Degree College (Affiliated to the University of Mumbai – College Code: MU-1283). Both are Government-Recognized Unaided Institutions focused on quality education and all-round student development.

Our Vision

To emerge as a leading educational institution in Maharashtra known for academic excellence, innovation, and social responsibility. We aim to build a seamless learning ecosystem that connects school, junior, and degree levels — guiding students to grow with confidence and pride.

Our Mission

  • Deliver holistic, value-based education through innovation and technology.
  • Encourage creativity, critical thinking, and collaboration in every learner.
  • Equip students with knowledge, ethics, and life skills for real-world success.
  • Foster an inclusive, inspiring, and supportive environment.

About the Trust

Jagdish Krishna Educational Society’s stands for academic excellence and community upliftment. Guided by visionary management, it works to shape future-ready citizens equipped to lead with knowledge, empathy, and integrity.
